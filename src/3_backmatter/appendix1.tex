The integral

\begin{equation}
\int_a^{b} \; f(x) \; dx = \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} \; \sum_{i = 0}^{n} \; f(x_i) \; \Delta x
\end{equation}

Equivalently

\begin{equation}
\int_a^{b} \; f(x) \; dx = \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} \; \sum_{i = 0}^{n} \; f(x_i) \; \Delta x
\end{equation}
